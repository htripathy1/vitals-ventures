Vitals & Ventures

You're the director of a community health clinic.
Treat patients. Manage the budget. Balance mission and margin.
Discover why healthcare finance is harder than it looks.

🩺
Treat Patients
Every quarter, patients arrive with different insurance statuses. Insured = profitable. Uninsured = full cost to you but maximum community impact. Same treatment, completely different economics.
💰
Manage the Budget
Pay staff, handle events, make strategic investments. Cash hits zero = clinic closes. Every dollar you spend is a dollar you can't spend elsewhere — that's opportunity cost.
⚡
Handle Curveballs
Flu surges, equipment failures, Medicaid rate cuts, natural disasters. Real healthcare finance is unpredictable. Your cash reserves are your defense.
⚖️
Balance the Score
Composite score = 40% Financial + 40% Community Health + 20% Reputation. Only maximizing one dimension fails. Multi-objective optimization is the lesson.

Finance Concepts Built Into Every Decision

Payer Mix
The ratio of insured vs. Medicaid vs. uninsured patients — your biggest revenue lever
Operating Margin
Revenue minus total costs per quarter. Negative = you're burning cash.
Opportunity Cost
Choosing one thing means giving up another. Every hire = equipment you can't buy.
Cash Runway
How many quarters until you run out of cash at your current burn rate.
Cross-Subsidy
Profits from insured patients fund care for uninsured ones. Balance is survival.
Revenue Diversification
Grants, insurance, Medicaid — three streams. Lose one and the others must compensate.
